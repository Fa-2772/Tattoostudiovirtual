NEW SCHOOL TATTOO

ORIGINS OF NEWSCHOOL

New schools foundation stems from ‘American Tradition’. The rules that were set about ageing tattoos and healthy tattoos stem from this style. Bold Black lines that help colour from spreading as well big shapes and ease of design that is legible from distance. Tattoos have a strong influence on Neo-traditional art. Such as Art Nouveau and Japanese aesthetic. This is seen through the method of block colouring.

TECHNIQUE AND FINISH

STYLES OF NEW SCHOOL

As mentioned previously the influence of neo-traditional styles (Art nouveau, and Japanese aesthetics), influences the style a lot. The influence of the Japanese aesthetic stems from gaming and comic book culture. This was due to the heavy access to the internet and the influence these genres have over pop culture and western media. During the 80s graffiti had a huge influence on this style, so the use of bright colours and drop shadows and fonts were incorporated into the design.
